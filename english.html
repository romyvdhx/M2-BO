History

Nxt delivers in a former production studio of 2100 square meters Museum a unique addition to the flourishing creative district of Amsterdam North. Inspired by the international museum landscape where interactive technology is driven Nxt wants to offer visitors a unique and often personalized experience at exhibitions Museum to be the pioneer in the field of emerging tech, new media art and visitor experience The Netherlands.

Nxt Museum, founded by the young entrepreneur Merel van Helsdingen, exhibits large-scale exhibits art installations, which are developed from interdisciplinary collaborations between established and emerging artists, designers, technicians, musicians and scientists.

Reviews

Van Lum

I would like to invite you to a wonderful place where light, the digital world and artificial intelligence play together. A fantastic spectacle! An amazing experience. There is also a restaurant in the museum, where they cook excellently! Perfect for a rainy day. Great staff. Thanks Sam.

Renata Repovska

I wanted to share my experience about my visit to the NXT museum. I went on tour with my teammates in November 2023 and it was breathtaking! The installations had interactive aspects that were fascinating and the music was stunning, which made the whole experience complete. We were also impressed by the staff who were very friendly and spoke perfect English. Moreover, the building, which used to be an old TV studio, suits the needs of the museum. I would definitely recommend this museum, not just to tech and media people, but to everyone. It was a unique and unforgettable experience.
